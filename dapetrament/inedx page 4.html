About the Department

The General Surgery department focuses on diagnosing and treating a wide range of surgical conditions, particularly those related to the abdomen and digestive system. It offers advanced surgical care with an emphasis on patient safety and recovery.


Our Services

  • Abdominal Surgery:Includes procedures like appendectomy and hernia repair.
  • Endocrine Surgery:Treats breast cancer or benign conditions.
  • Trauma Surgery:Emergency care for injuries from accidents.
  • Colorectal Surgery:Laparoscopic procedures for faster recovery.
