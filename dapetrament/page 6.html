Oncology

  

About the Department

Oncology is the medical field dedicated to the study, diagnosis, and treatment of cancer. It encompasses various specialties like medical, surgical, and radiation oncology to manage and research cancer effectively.


Our Services

  • Cancer Diagnosis: Biopsies, imaging, and laboratory tests to identify and stage cancer.
  • Treatment Options: Chemotherapy, radiation therapy, immunotherapy, targeted therapy, and surgery.
  • Supportive Care: Pain management, palliative care, and nutritional counseling.
  • Screening and Prevention: Regular screenings, genetic testing, and lifestyle guidance to reduce cancer risk.
